GoT enables novel transformations of thoughts thanks to the graph-based model for reasoning. We refer to them as graph-enabled transformations. For example, in writing, one could combine several input articles into one coherent summary. In sorting, one could merge several sorted subarrays of numbers into a final sorted array.
