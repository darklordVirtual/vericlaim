We focus our implementation on supporting efficient training of models with 100B parameters, which are an order-of-magnitude larger than the largest published models today (e.g., T5-11B [4]) while trainable within a reasonable time frame on current hardware (e.g., with 1K V100 GPUs). We implement and evaluate a subset of optimizations in ZeRO— in ZeRO-DP plus ZeRO-R — that allows us to achieve this goal. We will refer to this implementation as ZeRO-100B. Our results show that ZeRO-100B can efficiently train models with up to 170B parameters, 8x bigger than SOTA, up to 10x faster and with improved usability.
