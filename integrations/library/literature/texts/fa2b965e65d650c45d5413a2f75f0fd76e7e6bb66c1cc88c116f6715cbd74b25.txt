The strongest results of chain-of-thought prompting are summarized in Figure 4, with all experimental outputs for each model collection, model size, and benchmark shown in Table 2 in the Appendix. There are three key takeaways. First, Figure 4 shows that chain-of-thought prompting is an emergent ability of model scale (Wei et al., 2022b). That is, chain-of-thought prompting does not positively impact performance for small models, and only yields performance gains when used with models of 100B parameters.
