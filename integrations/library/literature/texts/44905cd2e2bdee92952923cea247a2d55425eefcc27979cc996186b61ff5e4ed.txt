Then there were 21 trees after some more were planted. So there must have been 21 - 15 = 6. The answer is 6. Q: Shawn has five toys. For Christmas, he got two toys each from his mom and dad. How many toys does he have now? A: Shawn started with 5 toys. If he got 2 toys each from his mom and dad, then that is 4 more toys. 5 + 4 = 9. The answer is 9. Q: Leah had 32 chocolates and her sister had 42. If they ate 35, how many pieces do they have left in total? A: Originally, Leah had 32 chocolates. Her sister had 42. So in total they had 32 + 42 = 74. After eating 35, they had 74 - 35 = 39. The answer is 39. ***** End In-Context Examples *****
