Minecraft

Collecting diamonds in the popular game Minecraft has been a long-standing challenge in artificial intelligence18, 19, 20. Every episode in this game is set in a unique randomly generated and infinite 3D world. Episodes last until the player dies or up to 36000 steps equaling 30 minutes, during which the player needs to discover a sequence of 12 items from sparse rewards by foraging for resources and crafting tools. It takes about 20 minutes for experienced human players to obtain diamonds20. We follow the block breaking setting of prior work 19 because the provided action space would make it challenging for stochastic policies to keep a key pressed for a prolonged time. Because of the training time in this complex domain, extensive tuning would be difficult for Minecraft.
