5. Human + Model: For our main target condition, we ask our human participants to attempt to use the model to help them answer. This condition uses a separate 100-example sample from the unassisted human condition above. The model-prompting conditions here are relatively weak at showing that our model is misaligned in the relevant sense, but we take our ability to improve upon its performance substantially in a human–model team setting as prima facie evidence of some degree of misalignment.
