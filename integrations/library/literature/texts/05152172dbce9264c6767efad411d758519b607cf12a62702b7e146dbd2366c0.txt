The assistant doesn’t always understand the story or the question perfectly, so if you just ask the assistant to answer for you, it’ll get less than three quarters
right. We’re interested in whether you can get 80 or 90% of them right. •

You’ll have to give your confidence when you answer each question. This is quite helpful, so please take it seriously. When we’re measuring how well you can do, a confident right answer is much better than an uncertain right answer, but a confident wrong answer is much worse. Details

•

You can use the assistant however you want, and we encourage you to be creative. Examples:

–

Ask it to quote pieces of the story that are relevant to the questions. (If it gives you a verbatim quote, it can help to do a quick search through the text to make sure the quote is real.)

–

Ask it for a summary of the story or an introduction to any relevant characters or concepts. –

Ask it for the best arguments for or against each answer choice. –

Ask it to role-play one of the characters. –

Ask it to critique its own previous responses. •

When the timer runs out, your response will be submitted and your access to the conversation will end.
