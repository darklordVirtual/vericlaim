We expect that sufficiently large models fine-tuned on any of these versions with sufficiently many examples will be robust to these changes in task formulation. Furthermore, recently published results on still larger models indicate that some of the phrasing sensitivity presented here may in fact be resolved at larger scales (Chowdhery et al., 2022) even in the few-shot setting.
