The distributions and overall conclusions are almost identical to Spearman. We also systematically catalog the feature output weight logits with Claude. We use the same explanation Claude generated to predict activations and provide 100 logits, 50 taken randomly and 50 which are the 10th–60th largest positive logits (the top 10 were used to generate the original explanations and thus held out). Claude was asked to output a binary label for if this logit would plausibly come after the feature fired, given the explanation. Note that the chance performance is 50%.
