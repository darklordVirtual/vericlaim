Current directions in interpretability research

Building more interpretable models. A separate but complementary line of work seeks to build models that are constrained to be interpretable by design (as opposed to training a complex, hard-to-interpret model and then attempting to analyze it post-hoc with one of the above techniques) [211] [212] [208] [213] [214] [215] [216].
