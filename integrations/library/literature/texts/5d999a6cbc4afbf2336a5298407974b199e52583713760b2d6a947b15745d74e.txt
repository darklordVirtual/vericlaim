We see that the feature was causally helping the model predictions on Arabic script through that full range, and the only tokens made less likely by the feature are punctuation shared with other scripts. The magnitudes of the impact are larger when the feature is more active. We encourage interested readers to view the feature visualization for A/1 to review this and other effects. We also validate that the feature's downstream effect is in line with our interpretation as an Arabic script feature by sampling from the model with the feature activity "pinned" at a high value.
