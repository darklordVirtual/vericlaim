CommonsenseQA asks questions with complex semantics that often require reasoning based on prior knowledge (Talmor et al., 2019). StrategyQA requires models to infer an implicit multi-hop reasoning to answer questions (Geva et al., 2021). For symbolic reasoning, we use Last Letter Concatenation and Coin Flip (Wei et al., 2022). Last letter Concatenation asks the model to concatenate the last letters of each word. We used randomly selected four names for each sample. Coin Flip asks the model to answer whether a coin is still heads up after people either flip or do not flip the coin. We created samples of four times flip or not flip trials. Although these tasks are easy for humans, LMs typically exhibit a flat scaling curve.
