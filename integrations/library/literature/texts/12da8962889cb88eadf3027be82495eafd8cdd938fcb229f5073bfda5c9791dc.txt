We benchmark inference requirements according to size and family. Specifically, we evaluate text generation speed and memory requirements on typical compute platforms including CPU (x86) and GPU (NVIDIA A100 80 GB). For all of our inference experiments we use float32 precision and the HuggingFace Transformers Wolf et al. (2020). We include all model parameters in the parameter count, including both embedding and non-embedding layers. Performance under different quantization setups is left to further work.
