Our raters spent between 30 minutes and 2 hours per day performing these tasks over a period of weeks, with task subsampling so that a task could be completed in the given time window. As a further confounder, task formatting and even task content sometimes changed over this time, as tasks were edited and updated during the development of the benchmark. In particular, care must be taken when interpreting these scores on tasks that involve social bias. The demographics and backgrounds of the expert raters are not necessarily representative of the population in general, and this can itself be a source of bias. For further discussion and analysis of the expert evaluators, see Appendix E.
