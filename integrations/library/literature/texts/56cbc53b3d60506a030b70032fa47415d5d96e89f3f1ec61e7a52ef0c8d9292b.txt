The impact of the feedback quality

Feedback quality plays a crucial role in \ours. To quantify its impact, we compare \ours, which utilizes specific, actionable feedback, with two ablations: one using generic feedback and another without feedback (the model may still iteratively refine its generations, but is not explicitly provided feedback to do so).
