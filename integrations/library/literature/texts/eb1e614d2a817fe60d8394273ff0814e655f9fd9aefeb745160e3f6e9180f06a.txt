Scaling up advantages when rewards are sparse can amplify noise that outweighs the entropy regularizer and stagnates exploration. Normalizing rewards or returns by standard deviation can fail under sparse rewards where their standard deviation is near zero, drastically amplifying rewards regardless of their size. Constrained optimization targets a fixed entropy on average across states 32, 33 regardless of achievable returns, which is robust but explores slowly under sparse rewards and converges lower under dense rewards. We did not find stable hyperparameters across domains for these approaches. Return normalization with a denominator limit overcomes these challenges, exploring rapidly under sparse rewards and converging to high performance across diverse domains.
