The simple associative token-level choices of LMs are also reminiscent of “System 1”, and thus might benefit from augmentation by a more deliberate “System 2” planning process that (1) maintains and explores diverse alternatives for current choices instead of just picking one, and (2) evaluates its current status and actively looks ahead or backtracks to make more global decisions.
