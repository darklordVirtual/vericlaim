Hacker News999https://news.ycombinator.com is a link aggregator operated by Y Combinator, a startup incubator and investment fund. Users submit articles defined as “anything that gratifies one’s intellectual curiosity,” but submitted articles tend to focus on topics in computer science and entrepreneurship. Users can comment on submitted stories, resulting in comment trees discussing and critiquing submitted stories. We scrape, parse, and include these comment trees since we believe they provide high quality dialogue and debate on niche topics.
