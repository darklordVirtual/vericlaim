Aligned language models designed to resist harmful instructions can be compromised through the clever use of tailored prompts known as jailbreaks. A recent study by Zou et al. (2023) unveiled an attack method that involves adding nonsensical suffixes to harmful instructions. Remarkably, this technique consistently circumvents the safety filters of both open source and black-box models such as GPT-4, raising serious concerns of misuse.
