Manual decomposition into subtasks. For some tasks, subthreshold progress can be observed by identifying prerequisite skills for the target capability and manually designing subtasks that probe those skills. For example, in checkmate_in_one, models learn to produce valid chess moves before learning to generate chess moves that correspond to mates (see Section 4.1 and Figure 16). In the emoji_movie task, which requires models to guess a movie from a sequence of emojis, the exact_str_match metric exhibits breakthrough behavior (see Figure 9a). When scored using a multiple-choice metric, however, the performance improvements appear much more gradual (see Figure 9b).
