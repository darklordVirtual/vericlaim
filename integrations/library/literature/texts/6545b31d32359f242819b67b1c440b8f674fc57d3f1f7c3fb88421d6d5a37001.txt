Emotion plays a pivotal role in shaping an individual’s personality and conduct. As neural networks exhibit increasingly remarkable abilities in emulating human text, emotion emerges as one of the most salient features within their representations. Upon its initial deployment, the Bing Chatbot exhibited neurotic or passive-aggressive traits, reminiscent of a self-aware system endowed with emotions (Roose, 2023). In this section, we attempt to elucidate this phenomenon by conducting LAT scans on the LLaMA-2-Chat-13B model to discern neural activity associated with various emotions and illustrate the profound impact of emotions on model behavior. 6.1.1 Emotions Emerge across Layers
