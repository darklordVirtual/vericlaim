It shall apply from 2 August 2026. However:

(a)

Chapters I and II shall apply from 2 February 2025;

(b)

Chapter III Section 4, Chapter V, Chapter VII and Chapter XII and Article 78 shall apply from 2 August 2025, with the exception of Article 101;

(c)

Article 6(1) and the corresponding obligations in this Regulation shall apply from 2 August 2027. This Regulation shall be binding in its entirety and directly applicable in all Member States. Done at Brussels, 13 June 2024. For the European Parliament
