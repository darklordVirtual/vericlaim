3.1.4 StoryCloze

We next evaluate GPT-3 on the StoryCloze 2016 dataset [83], which involves selecting the correct ending sentence for five-sentence long stories. Here GPT-3 achieves 83.2% in the zero-shot setting and 87.7% in the few-shot setting (with ). This is still 4.1% lower than the fine-tuned SOTA using a BERT based model [64] but improves over previous zero-shot results by roughly 10%.
