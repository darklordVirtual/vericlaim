Speech Commands

The Speech Commands (SC) dataset [47] is originally a 35-class dataset of spoken English words. However, this paper was part of a line of work starting with Kidger et al. [23] that has used a smaller 10-class subset of SC [23, 35, 18, 36]. In an effort to avoid dataset fragmentation in the literature, we have since moved to the original dataset. We are now calling this 10-class subset SC10 to distinguish it from the full 35-class SC dataset. To cite S4 as a baseline for Speech Commands, please use Table 11 from [19] instead of Fig. 6 from this paper. In addition to using the full SC dataset, it also provides a number of much stronger baselines than the ones used in this work. WikiText-103
