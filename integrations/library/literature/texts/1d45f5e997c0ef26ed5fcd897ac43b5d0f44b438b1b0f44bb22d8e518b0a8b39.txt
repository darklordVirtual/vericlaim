Note that we expect the overlap between the interference and base64 token logit weights to be from the aforementioned usage of base64 tokens across many other contexts. The Feature Is Not A Neuron

Looking at the neuron in model A that most correlates with this feature: A/neurons/470 (corr=0.18), we find that while it does notably respond to base64 strings, it also activates for lots of other things, including code, HTML labels, parts of URLs, etc.:

The logit weights suggest it somewhat increases base64 tokens, but is much more focused on upweighting other tokens, e.g. filename endings. The activation and logit correlations are consistent with this neuron helping represent the same feature, but largely doing other things.
