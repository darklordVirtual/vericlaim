The one-layer transformers we study are trained on the Pile . We chose to train these models on the Pile over Anthropic's internal dataset in order to make our experiments more reproducible. While we think many features are universal, others are very likely idiosyncratic to the dataset. We train the transformers on 100 billion tokens using the Adam optimizer . We hypothesize that a very high number of training tokens may allow our model to learn cleaner representations in superposition. These transformers have a residual stream dimension of 128, and an inner MLP dimension of 512. The MLP activation is a ReLU.
