These results illustrate two fundamental lessons in LM memorization. First, larger models memorize significantly more training data: even hundreds of millions of parameters are not enough to memorize some of the training points. The ability of LMs to improve with model size
has been extensively studied [35, 38]; we show a negative trend where these improvements come at the cost of decreased privacy. Second, for the largest LM,
