In the vicinity of 25-30% of data, a decrease of 1% of training data leads to an increase of 40-50% in median time to generalization. While the number of steps until validation accuracy 99% grows quickly as dataset size decreases, the number of steps until the train accuracy first reaches 99% generally trends down as dataset size decreases and stays in the range of - optimization steps. We’ve observed a similar pattern of exponential increase in optimization time until reaching generalization as dataset size decreases on all the algorithmic tasks for which we could get the networks to generalize.
