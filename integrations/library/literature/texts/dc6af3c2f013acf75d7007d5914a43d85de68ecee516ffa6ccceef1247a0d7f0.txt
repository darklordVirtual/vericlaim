It is evident that the true positive activation response in the active neurons is much higher than the false positive, which is expected to be reflected in the final softmax layer response. Moreover, it can be seen that the large activation values are spread over many neurons, indicating that
the confidence signal arises due to numerous patterns detected by neurons in this layer. Qualitatively similar behavior
