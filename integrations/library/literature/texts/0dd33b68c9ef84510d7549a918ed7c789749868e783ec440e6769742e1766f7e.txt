Transformers were proposed by Vaswani et al. [1] as an architecture to efficiently model sequential data. These models consist of stacked self-attention layers with residual connections. Each self-attention layer receives embeddings corresponding to unique input tokens, and outputs embeddings , preserving the input dimensions.
