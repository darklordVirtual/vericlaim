We seek to endow some pre-trained machine learning model with guarantees on its performance as to ensure its safe deployment. Suppose we have a base model that is a function mapping inputs to values in some other space, such as a probability distribution over classes. Our job is to design a procedure that takes the output of and post-processes it into quantities with desirable statistical guarantees.
