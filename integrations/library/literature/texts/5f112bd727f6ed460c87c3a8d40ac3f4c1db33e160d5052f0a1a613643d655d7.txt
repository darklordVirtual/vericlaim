The complete set of optimizations in ZeRO could allow us to run models with trillion parameters on the high-end hardware cluster today (e.g., with 1K V100 GPUs), however, the hardware compute capacity is still too limited and training time can be impractically long (1 year). Therefore, our focus for this implementation is to efficiently support models with 10x parameters (100B parameters) than state-of-the-art (SOTA) while still being within reach of the compute capabilities of current hardware.
