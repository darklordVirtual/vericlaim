The datasets we consider are binary operation tables of the form where are discrete symbols with no internal structure, and is a binary operation. Examples of binary operations include addition, composition of permutations, and bivariate polynomials. Training a neural network on a proper subset of all possible equations then amounts to filling in the blanks of the binary op table, much like solving a Sudoku puzzle.
