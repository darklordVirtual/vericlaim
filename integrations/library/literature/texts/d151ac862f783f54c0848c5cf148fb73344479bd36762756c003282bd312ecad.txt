It is thus critical to consider not only which claims can and can’t be substantiated with existing mechanisms in theory, but also who is well-positioned to scrutinize these mechanisms in practice.414141In Section 3.3, we discuss the role that computing power–in addition to expertise–can play in influencing who can verify which claims. Keeping their limitations in mind, software mechanisms can substantiate claims associated with AI development in various ways that are complementary to institutional and hardware mechanisms. They can allow researchers, auditors, and others to understand the internal workings of any given system. They can also help characterize the behavioral profile of a system over a domain of expected usage.
