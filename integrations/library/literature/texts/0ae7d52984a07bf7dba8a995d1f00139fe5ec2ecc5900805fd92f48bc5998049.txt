We present the training dynamics of Qwen3-VL-4B and Qwen3-VL-8B in Figure 14. As illustrated, training with visual certainty estimation exhibits fast initial convergence with ECE reducing to 0.1 in less than 100 steps, achieving higher calibration performance more efficiently. These results indicate that the proposed supervision signal not only improves calibration and reasoning accuracy, but also provides a strong and effective training signal.
