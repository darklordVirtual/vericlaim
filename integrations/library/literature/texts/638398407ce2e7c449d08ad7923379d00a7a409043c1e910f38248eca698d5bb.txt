Dataset Generation

Throughout all of our experiments, we use a subset of CCNet (Wenzek et al., 2020) as our language modeling dataset and GPT-J (Wang and Komatsuzaki, 2021) as our language model . To reduce the computational cost of annotating with API calls, we define heuristics for some APIs to get a subset of for which API calls are more likely to be helpful than for an average text. For example, we only consider texts for the calculator tool if they contain at least three numbers. Details of the heuristics used are given in Appendix A.
