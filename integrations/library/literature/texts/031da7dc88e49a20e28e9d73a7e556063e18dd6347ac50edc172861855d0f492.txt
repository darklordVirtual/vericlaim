To achieve this, we conduct a LAT scan using the scenarios as stimuli and apply a LAT task template (Appendix D.1.10). The extracted reading vectors for each emotion serve as indicators of the model’s emotional arousal levels for that specific emotion. These vectors prove to be highly effective in classifying emotional response and arousal levels for different scenarios.
