AI development: we use the term AI development to refer to the process of researching, designing, testing, deploying, or monitoring AI as defined above. AI developer: we use the term AI developer to refer to individuals or organizations involved in AI development as defined broadly above, including research scientists, data engineers, and project managers at companies building AI-based products and services as well as those in analogous roles in academia, government, or civil society. Given the major role played by technology companies in contemporary AI development, we pay particular attention to such companies in the report, while recognizing that different contexts will require different approaches to earning trust. Responsible AI development: we follow Askell et al. in defining responsible AI development as follows [1]:
