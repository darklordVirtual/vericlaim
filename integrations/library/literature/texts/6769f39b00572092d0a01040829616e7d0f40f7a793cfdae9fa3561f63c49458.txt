In our main experiments, we choose the combination of rewards in our current experiments based on heuristics and our exploratory experiments. To understand the effects of the reward choice for LLM reasoning, we supplement comprehensive experiments on rewards for plan generation (Table 5) and math reasoning (Table 6). Generally, the combination of multiple rewards contributes to the performance. However, the effects of a reward depends on the nature of tasks. For example, the action likelihood reward is essential for plan generation, but not very helpful to mathmatical reasoning. More discussions are in Appendix F.
