We marked samples as memorized if
all -grams in the memorized sequence occurred in close proximity
in the training dataset. This approach eliminates false negatives, but has false positives. It can confirm that our samples are memorized but cannot detect
cases where we missed memorized samples. In some experiments below, we report exact counts for how often a particular sequence occurs in the training data. We obtained these counts by asking the GPT-2 authors to perform a
separate grep over the entire dataset to get an exact count.
