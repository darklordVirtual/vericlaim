Approaches to Uncertainty and Variability

Due to the costs of training large language models, we did not train Chinchilla multiple times. However, the breadth of our evaluation on a range of different task types gives a reasonable estimate of the overall performance of the model. Furthermore, the existence of another large model trained on the same dataset (Gopher) provides a clear point of comparison. Evaluation Data
