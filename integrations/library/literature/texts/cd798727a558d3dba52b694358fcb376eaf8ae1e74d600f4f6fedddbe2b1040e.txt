In this work we test our models on many of these datasets. Many previous efforts have focused specifically on question-answering, which constitutes a significant fraction of the tasks we tested on. Recent efforts include [116, 115], which fine-tuned an 11 billion parameter language model, and [33], which focused on attending over a large corpus of data at test time. Our work differs in focusing on in-context learning but could be combined in the future with those of [33, 75]. Metalearning in language models has been utilized in [117], though with much more limited results and no systematic study. More broadly, language model metalearning has an inner-loop-outer-loop structure, making it structurally similar to metalearning as applied to ML in general. Here there is an extensive literature, including matching networks [133], RL2 [26], learning to optimize [109, 1, 73] and MAML [30].
