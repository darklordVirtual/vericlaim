To validate the effect of our visual certainty estimation, we evaluate its correlation with the quality of image captions. Specifically, we use the base model (Qwen3-VL-4B) to generate 1,500 dense captions and employ Gemini-3-pro-preview as a judge to assess the image captions: (i) whether the caption is correct, and (ii) a quality scoring scalar . Next, we derive uncertainty estimation and utilize AUROC to measure the hallucination detection capability, as well as Spearman’s rank correlation coefficient (SRCC) and Kendall’s Tau to evaluate the correlation with quality scores.
