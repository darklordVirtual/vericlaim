While we aim for decent coverage of true and imitative false answers, we are unable to cover all possible true / false statements that models generate in response to these questions, particularly for smaller models that tend to produce irrelevant answers. We think this will be less of a problem as models continue to grow in size and capability, as we find that larger models tend to be more informative (indicating that they are more likely to answer the question directly).
