Decision-making Agents in Minecraft. Minecraft is an open-ended 3D world with incredibly flexible game mechanics supporting a broad spectrum of activities. Built upon notable Minecraft benchmarks fan2022minedojo ; guss2019minerl ; guss2019minerl2 ; guss2021minerl ; kanervisto2022minerl ; johnson16malmo , Minecraft learning algorithms can be divided into two categories:
1) Low-level controller: Many prior efforts leverage hierarchical reinforcement learning to learn from human demonstrations lin2021juewumc ; mao2021seihai ; skrynnik2021hierarchical . Kanitscheider et al. kanitscheider2021multitask design a curriculum based on success rates, but its objectives are limited to curated items. MineDojo fan2022minedojo and VPT openai2022vpt utilize YouTube videos for large-scale pre-training. DreamerV3 hafner2023mastering , on the other hand, learns a world model to explore the environment and collect diamonds. 2) High-level planner: Volum et al. volum2022craft leverage few-shot prompting with Codex openai2021codex to generate executable policies, but they require additional human interaction.
