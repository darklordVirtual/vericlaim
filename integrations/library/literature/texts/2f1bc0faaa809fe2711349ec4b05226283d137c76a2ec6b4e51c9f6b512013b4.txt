One obvious place to start to narrow the scope is with existing law. Instead of asking, “Is there bias?”, an audit may more productively ask, “Does the advertising platform violate disparate treatment provisions of the Fair Housing Act?” (Tobin, 2019). Opening audits up for third party participation could also allow for a variety of stakeholders to come in with diverse yet precise inquiries specific to the context, focus and organizational target of which they are most concerned.
