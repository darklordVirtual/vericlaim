In this case, we evaluate the Retrieval-Augmented Chat’s end-to-end question-answering performance using the Natural Questions dataset Kwiatkowski et al. (2019). We collected 5,332 non-redundant context documents and 6,775 queries from HuggingFace. First, we create a document collection based on the entire context corpus and store it in the vector database. Then, we utilize Retrieval-Augmented Chat to answer the questions.
