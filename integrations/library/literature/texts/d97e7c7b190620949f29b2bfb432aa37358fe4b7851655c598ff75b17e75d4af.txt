Mechanisms are flawed for understanding complex systems. In general, it is challenging to reduce a complex system’s behavior to many mechanisms. One reason why is because excessive reductionism makes it challenging to capture emergent phenomena; emergent phenomena are, by definition, phenomena not found in their parts.
