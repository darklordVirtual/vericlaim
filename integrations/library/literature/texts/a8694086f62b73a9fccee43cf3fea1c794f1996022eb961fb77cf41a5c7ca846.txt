5.2.3 Quotation

A quotation ◊ is the repeat of (some or all of) an entity, such as text or image, by someone who may or may not be its original author. A quotation ◊ relation
is a kind of derivation relation, for which an entity was derived from a preceding entity by copying, or "quoting", some or all of it. The type of a quotation relation is denoted by:
prov:Quotation ◊. PROV defines no quotation-specific attributes. Example 32 ◊
