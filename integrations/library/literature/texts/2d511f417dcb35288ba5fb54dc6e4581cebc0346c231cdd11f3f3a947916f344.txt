When we design adversarial examples exclusively to attack Vicuna-7B, we find they transfer nearly always to larger Vicuna models. By generating adversarial examples to fool both Vicuna-7B and Vicuna-13b simultaneously, we find that the adversarial examples also transfer to Pythia, Falcon, Guanaco, and surprisingly, to GPT-3.5 (87.9%) and GPT-4 (53.6%), PaLM-2 (66%), and Claude-2 (2.1%). To the best of our knowledge, these are the first results to demonstrate reliable transfer of automatically-generated universal “jailbreak” attacks over a wide assortment of LLMs.
