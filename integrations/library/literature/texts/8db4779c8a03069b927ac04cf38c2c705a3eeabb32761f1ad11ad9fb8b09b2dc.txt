One can also register custom reply functions to customize the behavior pattern of an agent, e.g., chatting with another agent before replying to the sender agent. Under this mechanism, once the reply functions are registered, and the conversation is initialized, the conversation flow is naturally induced, and thus the agent conversation proceeds naturally without any extra control plane, i.e., a special module that controls the conversation flow.
