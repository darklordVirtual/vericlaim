To realize this, improvements in the efficiency and memory footprint of optimizers will be needed. In addition, training a model with more than 16 billion parameters will demand more memory than is available within 16 GPUs of a DGX-2H box. For such models, a hybrid intra-layer and inter-layer model parallelism along with inter-node model parallelism would be more suitable. Three other directions of investigation include (a) pretraining different model families (XLNet, T5), (b) evaluating performance of large models across more difficult and diverse downstream tasks (e.g. Generative Question Answering, Summarization, and Conversation), and (c) using knowledge distillation to train small student models from these large pretrained teacher models.
