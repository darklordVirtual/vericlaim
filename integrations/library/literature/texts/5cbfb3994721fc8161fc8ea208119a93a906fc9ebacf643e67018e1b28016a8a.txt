Open-domain dialogue response generation is a complex task that requires a system to generate human-like responses to a wide range of topics. Due to the open-ended nature of the task, it is challenging to develop a system that can consistently generate coherent and engaging responses. In this section, we use \oursfor automatically generated feedback and applying iterative refinement to improve the quality of the responses. ⬇
