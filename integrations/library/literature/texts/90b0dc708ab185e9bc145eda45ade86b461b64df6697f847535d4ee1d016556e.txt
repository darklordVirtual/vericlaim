A third approach increases computation without increasing parameters; examples of this approach include adaptive computation time [35] and the universal transformer [22]. Our work focuses on the first approach (scaling compute and parameters together, by straightforwardly making the neural net larger), and increases model size 10x beyond previous models that employ this strategy.
