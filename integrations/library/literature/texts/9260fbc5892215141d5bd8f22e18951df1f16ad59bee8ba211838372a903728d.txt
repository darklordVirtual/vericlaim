Notwithstanding some challenges associated with open source projects, they are uniquely amenable to broad-based scrutiny and iteration, and have yielded benefits already. Benchmarks for PPML could help unify goals and measure progress across different groups.636363The use of standard tools, guides, and benchmarks can also potentially advance research in other areas, but we focus on privacy-preserving ML in particular here given the backgrounds of the authors who contributed to this subsection.
