Superposition and attempts to resolve it have deep connections to many lines of research, including general investigations of interpretable features, linear probing, compressed sensing, dictionary learning and sparse coding, theories of neural coding, distributed representations, mathematical frames, vector symbolic architectures, and much more. Rather than attempt to do justice to all these connections here, we refer readers to the related work section of Toy Models of Superposition where we discuss these topics in depth, and also to our essay Distributed Representations: Composition & Superposition .
