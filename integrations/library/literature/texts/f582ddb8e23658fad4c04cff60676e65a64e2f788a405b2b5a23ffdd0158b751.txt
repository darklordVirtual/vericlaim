We evaluate Voyager systematically against other LLM-based agent techniques (e.g., ReAct yao2022react , Reflexion shinn2023reflexion , AutoGPT autogpt ) in MineDojo fan2022minedojo , an open-source Minecraft AI framework. Voyager outperforms prior SOTA by obtaining more unique items, unlocking key tech tree milestones up to faster, and traversing longer distances. We further demonstrate that Voyager is able to utilize the learned skill library in a new Minecraft world to solve novel tasks from scratch, while other methods struggle to generalize.
