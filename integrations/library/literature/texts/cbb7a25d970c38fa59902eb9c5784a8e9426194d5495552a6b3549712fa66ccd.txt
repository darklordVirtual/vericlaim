We propose three tasks that are hard even when sampling from the state-of-the-art language model, GPT-4 (OpenAI, 2023), using standard IO prompting or chain-of-thought (CoT) prompting. We show how deliberate search in trees of thoughts (ToT) produces better results, and more importantly, interesting and promising new ways to use language models to solve problems requiring search or planning.
