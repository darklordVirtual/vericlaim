Although we find that chain-of-thought reasoning is mostly correct for math questions where the final answer was correct, this is likely because it is hard to arrive at the correct final answer by coincidence for free response questions. For multiple choice or binary classification problems, it was much more likely that models could arrive at the correct answer via an incorrect reasoning path (e.g., all the commmonsense reasoning datasets we evaluate).
