We do not have a strong hypothesis for which direction should yield higher pass rates. While generating docstrings may be more forgiving because natural language syntax is less strict than code syntax, docstrings in our dataset may be lower quality because developers tend to devote less time to writing docstrings. Indeed, our model produces docstrings like “I just found this function online” and “This test is not correctly written and it’s not my solution.”
