Transparency can benefit from the open publication of AI systems (including code, data, and models), though privacy, safety, and competitive considerations prevent this from being appropriate in all cases.888888The necessity and sufficiency of transparency as an ideal for technical systems has also been critiqued in recent years, such as from Ananny and Crawford [130] and Kroll et. al. Trust and trustworthiness: these concepts have been extensively explored by researchers, though a consensus account across domains remains elusive.
