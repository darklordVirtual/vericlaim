Compute Power Gap Training a trillion parameter model end-to-end within an acceptable time range, however, could still require significant amount of compute power, which is lacking in today’s AI clusters. To understand the resource requirement, we present a brief comparison with Bert-Large. Bert-Large can be trained in minutes on a GPU DGX-2H cluster [26]. A 1 Trillion Parameter model can easily contain x (1 trillion / 330 million) more computation than a Bert-Large model for a data sample.
