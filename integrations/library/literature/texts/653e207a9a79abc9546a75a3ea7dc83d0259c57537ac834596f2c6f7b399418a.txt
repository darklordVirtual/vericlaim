For both 1.3B and 6B models, we scan the learning rate in log-linear space, from 2.55e-6 to 2.55e-5, for both PPO with and without the pretraining data mix. All runs with learning rate greater than 8.05e-6 diverged, for PPO models without pretraining data mix. For the 175B models, we did similar experiments with two learning rates of 2.55e-6 and 3.74e-06, due to compute constraints. Figure 38 shows the human evaluation results. PPO with pretraining data mix appears to be less sensitive to change of the learning rate. Based on these results, we picked the checkpoints with
the highest likert scores, as our final models. Figure 38: Human evaluation metrics as a function of learning rates.
