Algorithmic Learning in a Random World [vovk2005algorithmic], by Vovk, Gammerman, and Glenn Shafer, contains further perspective on the history described above in the bibliography of Chapter 2 and the main text of Chapter 10. Also, the book’s website links to several dozen technical reports on conformal prediction and related topics. We now help the reader understand some of these key developments.
