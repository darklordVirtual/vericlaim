Anthropomorphism shapes trust indirectly and conditionally rather than acting as a uniform amplifier.We organise these findings within a proposed Personality–Trust Calibration (PTC) framework and extend it to agentic LLMs, where the relevant failure mode shifts from over-reliance on advice to over-delegation of action. The framework is offered as a structured research agenda rather than a validated causal model.
