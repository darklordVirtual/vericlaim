Problem-solving success rate: Results from the quantitative evaluations show that AutoGen can help achieve the highest problem-solving success rate among all the compared methods. Based on the qualitative evaluation, we analyze the user experience concerning the verbosity of the response and the ability of the LLM-based system to run without unexpected behaviors. ChatGPT+Plugin is the least verbose, mainly because Wolfram queries are much shorter than Python code.
