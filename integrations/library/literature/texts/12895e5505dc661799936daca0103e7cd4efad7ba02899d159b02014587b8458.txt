We use a dropout probability of 0.1 on all layers. We use a gelu activation Hendrycks and Gimpel (2016) rather than the standard relu, following OpenAI GPT. The training loss is the sum of the mean masked LM likelihood and the mean next sentence prediction likelihood. Each pre-training took 4 days to complete. Longer sequences are disproportionately expensive because attention is quadratic to the sequence length. To speed up pretraing in our experiments, we pre-train the model with sequence length of 128 for 90% of the steps. Then, we train
the rest 10% of the steps of sequence of 512 to learn the positional embeddings.
