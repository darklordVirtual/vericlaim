Figure App.2 shows the percentage of tasks in BIG-bench ( axis) that receive a normalized score above a certain value ( axis), for different BIG-G model scales. For small model sizes most tasks achieve very low scores, while at the largest scale about half the tasks achieve a non-negligible score of 10 or higher. These results indicate that the improvement in performance at larger scale observed in Figure 1 is due to an overall improvement in performance across many tasks, and is not dominated by a few outliers.
