We also propose a general-purpose activation-based confidence, utility, and trust estimation protocol (acute) to appropriately adjudicate uncertainty. The acute protocol provides flexible, sample-efficient, and compute-efficient confidence estimators for 3 tasks including multiple choice question answering, tool-calling, and scientific document summarization across 6 models from 4 model families. acute outperforms strong baselines on euro, while maintaining low calibration error. Taken together, our work shows that equipping LLMs with the acute protocol can improve calibration, utility, and trustworthiness in numerous settings.
