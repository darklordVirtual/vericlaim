the data with transformations such as translations that are expected to actually occur in the
test set. This form of data augmentation instead uses inputs that are unlikely to occur naturally
but that expose flaws in the ways that the model conceptualizes its decision function. At the time, this procedure was never demonstrated to improve beyond dropout on a state of the art benchmark.
