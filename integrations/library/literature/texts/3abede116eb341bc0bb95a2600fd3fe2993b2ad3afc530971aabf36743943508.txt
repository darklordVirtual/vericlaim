The actor learns to choose actions that maximize return while exploring through an entropy regularizer 30. However, the correct scale for this regularizer depends both on the scale and frequency of rewards in the environment. Ideally, we would like the agent to explore more if rewards are sparse and exploit more if rewards are dense or nearby. At the same time, the exploration amount should not be influenced by arbitrary scaling of rewards in the environment.
