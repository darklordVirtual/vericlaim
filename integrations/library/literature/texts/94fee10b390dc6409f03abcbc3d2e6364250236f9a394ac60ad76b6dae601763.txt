FAA Office of Audit and Evaluation

The U.S. Federal Aviation Administration (FAA)’s Office of Audit and Evaluation (AAE) receives protected disclosures via its online hotline from FAA employees, certificate holders, an employees of certificate holders concerning potential violations of the FAA’s statute and regulations and acts, omissions, or gross misconduct that affect aviation safety. AAE evaluates these disclosures, and if it determines that further investigation is warranted, it will either refer the disclosure to another FAA office or request assistance to conduct an inquiry. HHS nursing home inspections

To participate in the Medicaid and Medicare programs, nursing homes must undergo annual health inspections.
