Finally, we propose a new metric for evaluating a prompting strategy, the volume of a thought (contribution #5). With this metric, we aim to understand better the differences between prompting schemes. For a given thought , the volume of is the number of LLM thoughts, from which one can reach using directed edges. Intuitively, these are all the LLM thoughts that have had the potential to contribute to . We show that GoT, by incorporating thought transformations such as aggregation, enables thoughts to have fundamentally larger volumes than other schemes.
