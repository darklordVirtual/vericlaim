Area under the euro curve

Since euro is parametrized by , each value is a setting for the net utility of correctly abstaining from trusting an incorrect candidate. We can compute the euro for each of these, which traces a curve. To compare confidence estimators across all possible settings, we summarize euro by taking the area under this curve and term this auc-euro.
