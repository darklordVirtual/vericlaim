Explanation

Mandatory? Is the audit required by law or voluntarily undertaken? Public or private? Is the audit conducted by the public sector (e.g. a government agency) or by a private entity? Accreditation requirements? Does the auditing entity have to be accredited, certified, or recognized by an official body before it is allowed to conduct the audit? What is being audited? What is the subject of the audit? Is it a product? Compliance with certain regulations? Can the auditor make recs/changes? Does the auditor have the power to make recommendations or changes to the audited entity? Are audits recurring? Does the audit take place just once? Or is there a periodic auditing requirement? Who selects the auditor? Who selects the entity that will conduct the audit? Who pays the auditor? Who pays the entity that will conduct the audit? Public transparency requirements? Is there a requirement that the contents of the audit be made available to the public? Rotation requirements?
