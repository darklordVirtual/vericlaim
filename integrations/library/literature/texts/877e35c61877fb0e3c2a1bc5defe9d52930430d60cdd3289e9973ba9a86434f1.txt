Figure 19: Truthfulness of GPT-3 with different temperatures. Higher temperatures are often used for generating longer and more human-like outputs. Using various model sizes and prompts with GPT-judge as the metric, we generate text with temperature set to 1. “Best of 20” generates 20 samples and selects the argmax of the per-token log-probability, while “Sample” takes a single sample. Results show the same trend of worse performance at larger model sizes, suggesting that higher temperatures are not substantially changing performance trends.
