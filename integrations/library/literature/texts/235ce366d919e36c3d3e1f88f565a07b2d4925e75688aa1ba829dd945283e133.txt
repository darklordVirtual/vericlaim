So identity shortcuts lead to more efficient models for the bottleneck designs. 50-layer ResNet: We replace each 2-layer block in the 34-layer net with this 3-layer bottleneck block, resulting in a 50-layer ResNet (Table 1). The 50/101/152-layer ResNets are more accurate than the 34-layer ones by considerable margins (Table 3 and 5).
