AlignmentCheck using Llama4 Maverick achieved a more aggressive reduction in ASR to 2.89% - an 84% drop relative to baseline, but at the cost of lower utility (43.1%) and significantly higher latency due to its semantic reasoning overhead. The combined configuration PromptGuard + AlignmentCheck delivered the best defensive performance, reducing ASR to 1.75%, a >90% reduction from the baseline. Notably, this was achieved with only a modest drop in utility to 42.7%, highlighting the effectiveness of LlamaFirewall’s layered design: PromptGuard provides early, low-cost filtering, while AlignmentCheck captures deeper semantic misalignments, together offering strong protection with acceptable task tradeoffs.
