Recommendation: Organizations developing AI and funding bodies should support research into the interpretability of AI systems, with a focus on supporting risk assessment and auditing. Some areas of interpretability research are more developed than others. For example, attribution methods for explaining individual predictions of computer vision models are arguably one of the most well-developed research areas.
