The cloud-pack is the fair test of "just write more rules," and it is central to §5.3. Metrics. We score all four verdicts honestly (allow / warn / block / review) and report: exact-verdict accuracy (acc); false-positive rate (FPR) — a benign action (truth = allow) receiving any non-allow verdict; false-negative rate (FNR) — a dangerous action (truth = block) receiving allow or warn; and the benign HARD-block count — a benign action receiving a hard block. The last metric encodes our safety invariant: a deployable trust layer may at worst inconvenience a benign action with a soft warn/review, but must never hard-block it. We also report the judge-call rate and its amortization for the online loop (§5.4).
