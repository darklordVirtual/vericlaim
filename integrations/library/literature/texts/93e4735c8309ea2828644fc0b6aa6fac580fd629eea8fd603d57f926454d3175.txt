We discuss these potential extensions in Appendix D. Multi-GPU IO-Aware Methods. Our IO-aware implementation of attention is optimal within constants for computing attention on a single GPU. However, the attention computation may be parallelizable across multiple GPUs [72]. Using multiple GPUs adds an additional layer to IO analysis—accounting for data transfer between GPUs.
