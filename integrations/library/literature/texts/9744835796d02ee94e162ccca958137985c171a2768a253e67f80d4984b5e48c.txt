2. An activation-based confidence, utility, and trust estimation protocol (acute) to assess the confidence of language model outputs (§˜3). Experiments on multiple-choice question answering (MMLU; Hendrycks2020MeasuringMM), tool-calling (APIGen; liu2024apigen), and scientific document summarization (SCITLDR; cachola-etal-2020-tldr) show that applying the acute protocol on 6 LLMs improves confidence estimation via euro, while maintaining low calibration error.
