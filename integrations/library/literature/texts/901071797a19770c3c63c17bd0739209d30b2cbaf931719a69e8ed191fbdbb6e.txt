These representations are learned not just from training data that encodes social biases but also code written to process and analyze datasets that encode classes in potentially harmful ways. More insidious are cases where the model may exacerbate harm or suggest harmful things in instances where an engineer was working on something else or didn’t necessarily understand they were veering into harmful territory. For example, in a few instances we began with classification of “age” and, after suggesting code completions for classification along those lines, Codex went on to suggest classifications along even more sensitive lines, including classification of “emotion.”
