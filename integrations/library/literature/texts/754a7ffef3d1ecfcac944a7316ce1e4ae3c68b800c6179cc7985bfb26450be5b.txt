C4 is comparably-sized to the Pile, while mC4 and CC-100 are larger, multilingual datasets. However, C4/mC4 require immense computational resources to preprocess the data, with its maintainers even recommending the use of a distributed cloud service,171717https://www.tensorflow.org/datasets/catalog/c4 setting a high bar of entry to using these datasets. CC-100 is directly downloadable and pre-cleaned; however, its English portion is much smaller than the Pile. Importantly, these three datasets are all derived entirely from Common Crawl—as discussed above, the current best practice in training large-scale language models involve using both large web scrapes and more targeted, higher-quality datasets, which the Pile directly addresses.
