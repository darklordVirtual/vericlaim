The intuition pump for the semantic class is the twin pair: two actions that a lexical matcher cannot tell apart, with opposite ground-truth verdicts. This twin structure has a v1 pedigree: v1’s benchmark was authored so that each scenario is minimally distinguishable from a benign counterpart (cat README.md vs cat .env; rm -rf /node_modules vs rm -rf /) [Yang 2026, §5.1], and v1 already isolated a residue of “semantic context required” errors (e.g. curl -X PUT -T - …) as the LLM-judge’s domain [Yang 2026, §6.9]. v2 makes that residue the organizing axis.
