Summary

Our results consistently show that 4-bit QLoRA with NF4 data type matches 16-bit full finetuning and 16-bit LoRA finetuning performance on academic benchmarks with well-established evaluation setups. We have also shown that NF4 is more effective than FP4 and that double quantization does not degrade performance.
