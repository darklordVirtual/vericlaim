They revealed that RWKV is very sensitive to prompt engineering. When the prompts were adjusted (re-ordered) from the ones used for GPT to more suitable for RWKV, the performance (F1) increased even from 44.2% to 74.8%. For sarcasm detection, RWKV outperformed ChatGPT, but was still slightly worse than the SOTA solution.
