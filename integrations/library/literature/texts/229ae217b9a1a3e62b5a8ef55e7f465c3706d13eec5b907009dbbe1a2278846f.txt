To complement the performance evaluations, we also perform an exploratory analysis of the text within the Pile to provide a detailed picture of the data. We hope that our extensive documentation of the construction and characteristics of the Pile will help researchers make informed decisions about potential downstream applications. Finally, we make publicly available the preprocessing code for the constituent datasets of the Pile and the code for constructing alternative versions222https://github.com/EleutherAI/the-pile.
