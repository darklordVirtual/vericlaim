Others have found that there exist vectors that loosely correspond to functions or tasks (hendel-etal-2023-context; todd2023function). Work combining mechanistic interpretability with calibration is rare: beigi-etal-2024-internalinspector use contrastive learning on model internals to improve trustworthiness, subramani-etal-2025-mice show that decoding from intermediate layers can improve calibration on tool-calling, and liu2025llm learn probes on activations to predict correctness in question-answering tasks. acute operationalizes the activation spaces to build compute- and sample-efficient confidence estimators to improve trustworthiness.
