COORDINATED CYBERSECURITY FRAMEWORKS

Article 7

National cybersecurity strategy

1. Each Member State shall adopt a national cybersecurity strategy that provides for the strategic objectives, the resources required to achieve those objectives, and appropriate policy and regulatory measures, with a view to achieving and maintaining a high level of cybersecurity.
