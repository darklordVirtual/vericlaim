Our findings suggest that the full architecture of generative agents generates the most believable behavior among all the conditions. We contrast the responses of the full architecture with those of other conditions below. However, we also report that the full architecture was not without flaws and illustrate its modes of failures. 6.5.1. The Full Architecture Bests Other Conditions

As seen in Figure 8, the full generative agent architecture produced the most believable behavior (; ).
