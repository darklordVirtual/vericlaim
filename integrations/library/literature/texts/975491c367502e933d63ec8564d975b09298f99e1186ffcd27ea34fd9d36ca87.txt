We stop the decay at a minimum learning rate of 1e-5. For BERT models, we largely follow the training process described in (Lan et al., 2019). We use the original BERT dictionary with vocab size of 30,522. In addition, we replace the next sentence prediction head with sentence order prediction as suggested by (Lan et al., 2019) and use whole word n-gram masking of (Joshi et al., 2019). For all cases, we set the batch size to 1024 and use a learning rate of 1.0e-4 warmed up over 10,000 iterations and decayed linearly over 2 million iterations. Other training parameters are kept the same as (Devlin et al., 2018).
