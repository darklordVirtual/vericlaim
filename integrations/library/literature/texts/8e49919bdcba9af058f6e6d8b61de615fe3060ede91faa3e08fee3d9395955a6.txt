in particular 1-D convolution kernels
which are the focus of our technical results in Section 3. Fig. 4 shows that S4 learns a variety of filters that display spatially consistent structure
and demonstrate awareness of the 2-D nature of the data. In particular, the lower layers learn simple kernels that extract features from just a few rows of local context while ignoring the rest of the image. On the other hand, higher layers aggregate information globally across full columns of the image at varying spatial frequencies. Filters in these higher layers span the entire context ( pixels), confirming S4’s ability to learn LRDs.
