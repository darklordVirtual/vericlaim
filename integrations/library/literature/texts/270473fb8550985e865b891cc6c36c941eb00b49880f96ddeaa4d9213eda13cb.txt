To demonstrate how emergent abilities can be induced by the researcher’s choice of metric, we show how to produce emergent abilities in deep networks of various architectures: fully connected, convolutional, self-attentional. We focus on vision tasks because abrupt transitions in vision models’ capabilities have not been observed to the best of our knowledge; this is one reason why emergence in large language models is considered so interesting.
