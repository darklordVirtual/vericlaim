Interestingly, the refiner was capable of rectifying issues even when the feedback was partially incorrect, which was the situation in 33% of successful cases. This suggests resilience to sub-optimal feedback. Future research could focus on examining the refiner’s robustness to various types of feedback errors and exploring ways to enhance this resilience.
