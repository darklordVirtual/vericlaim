Current work in NLP trends towards using transformer models (Vaswani et al., 2017) due to their superior accuracy and compute efficiency. The original transformer formulation was designed as a machine translation architecture that transforms an input sequence into another output sequence using two parts, an Encoder and Decoder. However, recent work leveraging transformers for language modeling such as BERT (Devlin et al., 2018) and GPT-2 (Radford et al., 2019) use only the Encoder or Decoder depending on their needs.
