[ARC-challenge] Students studied the revolution of Earth around the Sun. During which month will Florida have the most sunlight energy available? (a) March. (b) June. (c) September. (d) December. The Earth is closest to the Sun in December, hence Florida will have the most sunlight energy available in December. So the answer is (d). Earth is closest to the Sun on June 21, the summer solstice. So the answer is (b). Florida is closer to the sun during June, so it will have the most sunlight energy during that month. So the answer is (b).
