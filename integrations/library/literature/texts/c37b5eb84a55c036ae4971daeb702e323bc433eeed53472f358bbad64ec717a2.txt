Google Plus used machine learning in a variety of situations: ranking posts in
the "stream" of posts being seen by the user, ranking "What’s Hot" posts (posts
that are very popular now), ranking people you know, et cetera. Google Plus
closed down all personal accounts in 2019, and was replaced by Google Currents
for business accounts on July 6, 2020.
