We optimize the PRM at test time via search methods. We study three search approaches that sample outputs from a few-shot prompted base LLM (see Appendix G). An illustration is shown in Figure 2. Best-of-N weighted. We sample N answers independently from the base LLM and then select the best answer according to the PRM’s final answer judgement. Beam search.
