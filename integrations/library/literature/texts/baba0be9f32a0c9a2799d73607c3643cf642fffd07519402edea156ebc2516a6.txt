Our first tool is a question answering system based on another LM that can answer simple factoid questions. Specifically, we use Atlas (Izacard et al., 2022), a retrieval-augmented LM finetuned on Natural Questions (Kwiatkowski et al., 2019). Calculator

As a second tool, we use a calculator that can perform simple numeric calculations; we only support the four basic arithmetic operations. Results are always rounded to two decimal places. Wikipedia Search

Our third tool is a search engine that, given a search term, returns short text snippets from Wikipedia. Compared to our question answering tool, this search enables a model to get more comprehensive information on a subject, but requires it to extract the relevant parts by itself.
